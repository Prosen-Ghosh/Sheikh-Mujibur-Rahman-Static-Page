Sheikh Mujibur Rahman

Bangabandhu
Father of The Nation

My greatest strength is the love for my people, my greatest weakness is that I love them too much ---Sheikh Mujibur Rahman

Here's a time line of Sheikh Mujibur Rahman's life:

  • 1920 - Born in Tungipara, a village in Gopalganj District in the province of Bengal in British India.
  • 1929 - Mujib entered into class three at Gopalganj Public School, and two years later, class four at Madaripur Islamia High School.
  • 1940 - Mujib became politically active, he joined the All India Muslim Students Federation.
  • 1943 - He joined the Bengal Muslim League.
  • 1971 - First Presidents of Independent Bangladesh.
  • 1972 and 1975 - He was the leader of the Awami League.
  • 1975 - Dies at the age of 55 Dhaka, Bangladesh (He Was Killed By Pakistani Army).
